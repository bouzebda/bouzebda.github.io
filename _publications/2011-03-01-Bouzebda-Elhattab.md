### S. Bouzebda and I. Elhattab
We establish uniform-in-bandwidth consistency for kernel-type
estimators of the differential entropy. We consider two kernel-type estima-
tors of Shannon’s entropy. As a consequence, an asymptotic $$100\%$$ confi-
dence interval of entropy is provided.
