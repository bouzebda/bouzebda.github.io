### S. Bouzebda, A. Keziou and T. Zar
In this article we explore asymptotic properties of some statistics based on $$K$$-sample
extensions of multivariate empirical copula processes. These statistics can be used to test the
equality of copulas pertaining to $$K$$ independent samples.
