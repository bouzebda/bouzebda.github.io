### S. Bouzebda, N.E. El Faouzi and T. Zari
In this article, we establish optimal rates for the strong approximation of empirical
copula processes in $$\mathbb R^2$$ by sequences of Gaussian processes. These results are
applied to investigate Cramér–von Mises-type statistics.
